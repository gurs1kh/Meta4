META4

Objective:

In order to open the gate, obtain a key from each boss, who are located at the corners of the maps.

Then go through the gate to fight the final boss. He will spawn his minions for each of the 3 rounds of the fight and you must kill them all.

Controls:

  • Use WASD to move
  • Use the arrow keys to attack in the direction you choose
  • Press space to switch between melee weapon and bow
